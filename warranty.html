Free 30-Day Warranty

Warranty Policy

Your laptop comes with a free 30-day warranty starting from the day of purchase.

If the device fails on its own during normal use, we’ll take care of it.

If approved, you’ll receive a replacement device of the same model and performance. If that’s not available, you’ll receive WLR Laptops store credit up to the value of your original device.

This warranty covers failed devices — it is not a return policy and does not cover changing your mind.

What’s Covered

  • ✓ Hardware failures that occur during normal use
  • ✓ Defective internal components
  • ✓ Device no longer powering on or functioning correctly without misuse

What’s Not Covered

  • ✕ Batteries (considered consumable items)
  • ✕ Damage from drops, impacts, spills, liquid exposure, or rough handling
  • ✕ Damage caused by extreme temperatures, including cold weather
  • ✕ Unauthorized repairs, modifications, or tampering
  • ✕ Software issues, operating system problems, or user-installed programs

Warranty Terms & Details

This warranty applies to used devices only and is valid for 30 days from the original purchase date.

All warranty claims require the buyer to return the failed device to WLR Laptops. The device must be returned with all originally included accessories. Failure to return the device and accessories will void the warranty.

Devices must be dropped off by the buyer unless otherwise stated by WLR Laptops. Pick-up service is not provided by default.

Replacement devices will be of the same model and comparable performance. If an exact replacement is unavailable, store credit will be issued instead. No cash refunds are provided under this warranty.

Evidence of misuse, physical damage, liquid exposure, or environmental damage will void the warranty.
